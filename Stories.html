The Shiati’s Story: Unsung Heroes

PORTRAIT . Basam Orphan Foundation . November, 2024

The shiati’s

Ten-year-old Shiati has been through more than their fair share of challenges over the past decade. The countless health issues and medical procedures — from a feeding tube as an infant to surgery on his spinal cord — have made his life a veritable obstacle course. The root cause of the bone disease he has been diagnosed with remains a mystery to this day. Fortunately, with every new barrier that he has come up against, Sainte-Justine’s healthcare professionals have been right there by his side, giving him and his family the strength and the courage to carry on. And they can do that because of your generosity.
